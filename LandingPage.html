The Automotive Spotter

AR for Automotive Enthusiast

Download App

Automotive Spotter is an AR app that helps us automotive enthusiast learn about any car you see while out spotting. Turn it on and point to know not only know what kind of car you're looking at but the specifications of it as well - you can also capture photos of the cars and organize them to show off all the spots you've made! Collect and share the pictures with all you're automotive friends and maybe make some new ones while you're at it.

Automotive Spotter Main Features

Point to know

Capture the shot

Organize and Share
The Automotive Spotter Point to Know feature will analyze the image being seen by the camera and compare it to a large database of cars to determine what kind it is.
The Automotive Spotter Capture feature will allow you to capture the car to be organized and shared
The Automotive Spotter Organize and Share feature will allow you to organize all the spots you made based on chronological order as well as location. You'll also be able to create lists such as Exotics, Tuners, Muscle and so on. This feature will also give you easy access to sharing your spots with any fellow spotters using Automotive Spotter.

Design process

Design and development of Automotive Spotter
As a fellow automotive spotter and enthusiast I've talked with many other enthusiasts and we all agree on one thing. Theres no clear way to share the cars we're able to go out and see. Apps like Instagram and Snapchat make it easier but they don't provide good resources to organize cars we've spotted and none have features that allow you to quickly know what kind of car you're looking at or the specs it has.
